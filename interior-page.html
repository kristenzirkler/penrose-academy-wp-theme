How to Apply

Complete your Career Planning interview and get accepted at Penrose Academy. Discuss financial options with the Financial Aid Office.

Create an FSA ID, or update an existing outdated PIN, at https://fsaid.gov. This step is critical as the FSA ID gives you access to Federal Student Aid’s online systems and can serve as your legal signature.

Complete the FAFSA at www.fafsa.ed.gov. Our school code is 041753. Online instructions will guide you through the process.

Complete the Federal Direct Master Promissory Note (MPN). The MPN website allows you to complete and sign your MPN online. The process for completing this MPN is really easy; you’ll need your FSA ID to begin the MPN process.
